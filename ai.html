Case Study: A Bathroom Remodel Company's Facebook Growth

Challenge:

The brand had inconsistent posting, no clear social media strategy, and no existing assets to use for content. This made it difficult to engage their audience or grow their online presence.

Solution:

I developed a content strategy with clearly defined content pillars based on market research. Using free assets from the web, I created high-quality visuals and implemented a consistent weekly posting schedule. This approach allowed us to track what type of content resonated with the audience and optimize accordingly. Paid Meta ads were also used strategically to amplify reach and attract potential clients.

Results:

Over two and a half months, the brand experienced:

  • 7,000% increase in followers
  • 1,600% increase in content interactions
  • 200% increase in reach
  • 84.1% increase in website link clicks
  • 27.4% increase in video views

The strategy not only grew the brand’s audience but also drove meaningful engagement and website traffic, positioning the company as a trusted authority in bathroom remodeling.

Note: These results were achieved through a combination of organic content and strategically targeted Meta ads, not just organic growth.

Case Study: A Marketing Agency's Facebook Growth

Challenge:

The brand had inconsistent posting, no clear social media strategy, and only shared client results, wins, and testimonials. There was no consistent posting schedule, which limited engagement and audience growth.

Solution:

I developed a content strategy that positioned the brand not just as a marketing agency, but also as an educational resource for their audience. Content pillars were created to reframe client results, wins, and testimonials into educational posts while also addressing common objections from potential clients. High-quality visuals were designed, and a consistent weekly posting schedule was implemented to track what content performed best.

Results:

Over five months, the brand experienced:

  • 203.9% increase in views
  • 190.7% increase in reach
  • 140.8% increase in content interactions
  • 307.9% increase in link clicks, potentially bringing in new clients

The strategy not only grew the brand’s audience but also drove meaningful engagement and website traffic, positioning the company as a trusted authority in bathroom remodeling.

Note: These results were achieved through a combination of strategic content planning and consistent posting, not just organic sharing of client wins.
